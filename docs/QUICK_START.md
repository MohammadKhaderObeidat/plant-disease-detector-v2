# Quick Start Guide - 5 Minutes to Disease Detection

## 🚀 Get Started in 5 Steps

### Step 1: Open the Website (30 seconds)

Click this link:
```
https://MohammadKhaderObeidat.github.io/plant-disease-detector-v2/app.html
```

You should see a page with:
- Green header saying "Plant Disease Detector"
- Upload area on the left
- Results area on the right (empty initially)

---

### Step 2: Prepare Your Image (1 minute)

Take a photo of a plant leaf:

✅ **DO:**
- Use natural daylight
- Focus on the leaf
- Show the whole leaf if possible
- Use clear, sharp photo

❌ **DON'T:**
- Use blurry photos
- Take in dark lighting
- Include too much background
- Use very small leaf in frame

**Supported formats**: JPG, PNG, GIF (under 10 MB)

---

### Step 3: Upload the Image (1 minute)

**Option A - Click Upload:**
1. Click the **"Choose Image"** button
2. Select your leaf image from your computer
3. Wait for preview to appear

**Option B - Drag & Drop:**
1. Find your image file
2. Drag it to the upload area
3. Drop it in the box
4. Wait for preview to appear

You should see your image appear in the preview area.

---

### Step 4: Analyze (2 minutes)

1. Click the **"Analyze"** button (blue button)
2. Wait for the loading spinner to finish (about 2-3 seconds)
3. Results will appear on the right side

---

### Step 5: Read the Results (1 minute)

You'll see:

**Top Prediction:**
```
Disease Name: [e.g., "Rust"]
Confidence: [e.g., "92.5%"]
```

**What it means:**
- Higher percentage = More confident
- 90%+ = Very likely correct
- 50-70% = Probably correct
- Below 50% = Get a second opinion

**Treatment Recommendations:**
- List of actions to take
- How to treat the disease
- Prevention tips

## 🎓 Understanding Confidence Scores

### What is Confidence?

Confidence shows how sure the AI is about its prediction.

### Examples:

**95% Confidence** 🟢
- Very reliable
- Trust this result
- Take action based on it

**75% Confidence** 🟡
- Probably correct
- Could be close disease
- Verify if possible

**55% Confidence** 🟠
- Uncertain
- Get expert opinion
- Try different image

**Below 50%** 🔴
- Not confident
- Definitely get expert opinion
- Try better image

## 💡 Pro Tips

### Tip 1: Multiple Angles
Upload the same leaf from different angles for more confidence.

### Tip 2: Zoom In
Get a close-up shot of the affected area for better accuracy.

### Tip 3: Good Lighting
Natural daylight gives best results. Avoid shadows and artificial light.

### Tip 4: Clean Background
Simple background helps the AI focus on the leaf.

### Tip 5: Verify Results
Always verify with an expert for important decisions.
